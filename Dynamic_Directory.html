Interactive Office Map

How to use the interactive map:

  • Hover over sections of the map to find what you want
  • Click on a section to highlight the department or name in the directory below, or click on a conference room to open a calendar of the meetings scheduled in that room
  • You can switch which map you are using by clicking on the buttons below
    • By Department is a map of the departments on each floor
    • By Office is a map of each office on each floor
    • The Emergencies tab contains the evacuation/shelter maps for each respective emergency

How to use the directory:
